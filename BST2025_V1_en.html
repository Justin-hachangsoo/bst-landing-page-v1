7. Hybrid Staking System

BeautySkin AI (BST)'s Differentiated Hybrid Staking System

BST (BeautySkin AI) introduces an innovative hybrid staking system that provides both high reward rates and value stability. This system is based on real-time liquidity provision with USDT and offers differentiated high-yield rewards based on staking periods.

7.1 Staking Structure

BeautySkin AI's staking is structured as follows:

  • Basic Requirements: Provide BST coins and USDT to liquidity pool at real-time ratios
  • Minimum Staking Amount: Real-time ratio BST + 100 USDT
  • Principal Lock-up Period: 1 year (penalties apply for early withdrawal)
  • Reward Payment Cycle: Daily automatic payment
  • Reward Form: BST tokens

7.3 Hybrid Staking Process

  1. Liquidity Provision: Deposit BST and USDT into liquidity pool at real-time ratios
  2. Staking Model Selection: Choose between Basic (3 months), Premium (6 months), or Elite (12 months)
  3. Waiting Period: First-come, first-served basis limited by soft cap for selected model
  4. Reward Payment: Daily automatic reward payment begins after hybrid staking
  5. Compound Option: Re-staking available through automatic reinvestment
  6. Principal Release: Principal lock-up release and withdrawal available after model selection staking

7.4 Early Withdrawal and Penalties

If funds are withdrawn before the 1-year principal lock-up period, the following penalties apply:

  • 1-3 months after staking: 30% penalty on principal and interest
  • 4-6 months after staking: 20% penalty on principal and interest
  • 7-9 months after staking: 10% penalty on principal and interest
  • 10-12 months after staking: 5% penalty on principal and interest

Funds collected through penalties are used as an insurance fund to strengthen protocol stability.
